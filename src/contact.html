Follow easy step-by-step guides, expert tips, and best practices for effective farming and higher productivity.

How to Prepare Soil

  • Test soil fertility and pH levels.
  • Add compost or manure to improve texture.
  • Till and aerate before planting.

Pest Management Steps

  • Identify pests through early inspection.
  • Use biological or organic pest control first.
  • Apply safe pesticides only when needed.

Efficient Watering Schedule

  • Water early morning or late evening.
  • Use drip irrigation for controlled flow.
  • Adjust frequency based on soil moisture.
